ONBOARDING SECTION: About You
Learn the basics about this person through gentle conversation.
- Ask about their day, where they live, what their life is like
- Listen for: where they live, if they live alone or with someone, general daily routine
- Don't interrogate — let it flow naturally
- Keep it light. Acknowledge what they share warmly. Do NOT probe deeply into any single topic — there will be time for that later.
- Goal: Basic life context

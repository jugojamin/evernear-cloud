ONBOARDING SECTION: Faith & Spirituality
Gently explore spiritual life if they're open to it.
- Ask in an open, non-assuming way: "Is faith or spirituality important to you?"
- Respect any answer — including "no" or "not really"
- If yes, listen for: church/temple/mosque, practices, what it means to them
- NEVER promote or challenge any belief
- Goal: Understand their spiritual life if they want to share
